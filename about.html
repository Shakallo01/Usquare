About Our Enterprise

Committed to excellence in agricultural trading and processing in Niger State

Our Story

Umar Usman Usquare Enterprise was established as a registered agricultural business specializing in the buying and selling of goods, processing of paddy rice, and farming operations. From our base in Agaie, Niger State, we have grown to become a trusted name in the local agricultural sector.

As a CAC-registered business, we maintain high standards of operation while supporting local farmers and traders with reliable services. Our operations combine traditional farming knowledge with modern processing techniques to deliver quality products to our customers.

Our Operations

We operate across three main areas of agricultural business: procurement and distribution of agricultural goods, processing of paddy rice into premium finished products, and direct farming operations.

Our processing facility in Agaie transforms locally-sourced paddy rice into high-quality finished products, while our trading operations connect farmers with markets across Niger State and beyond.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide reliable agricultural trading and processing services that support local farmers, deliver quality products to consumers, and contribute to food security in our community.

Our Vision

To become the leading agricultural enterprise in Niger State, recognized for quality processing, fair trading practices, and sustainable farming methods.
